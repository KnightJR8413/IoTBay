Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Protecting Your Personal Data

Policy

IoTBay is highly committed to complying with Australian and international privacy laws and to protecting our customers’ privacy.

We collect details from our customers solely for processing orders more efficiently and to ensure you fully experience the benefits of our online ordering features.

All customer and payment data is securely processed and encrypted through trusted payment gateways to offer the highest level of protection possible. During transactions, IoTBay does not store sensitive payment details; if you use alternative payment methods, your personal information is handled in accordance with the respective provider’s standards.

IoTBay does not disclose, sell, or trade any collected information with third parties, except as necessary to verify orders or comply with legal obligations.

We do not reveal any details about our suppliers unless absolutely necessary.

Your contact information is shared with our delivery partners solely to keep you updated on your order status. You may review their privacy practices on their respective websites.

We use email and SMS notifications to keep you informed about your order status.

IoTBay will not use your details for unsolicited emails unless you have opted into our mailing list. We may use third-party services to collect feedback after your order is complete, and you can opt out at any time.

We provide your details to the appropriate authorities if the information provided is found to be false or misleading.

For quality, security, and training purposes, IoTBay may record phone conversations. Please inform the operator if you do not wish to be recorded.

Disclaimer

We are not responsible for typographical errors. Manufacturer specifications may change without notice as products are improved; consequently, information, images, and hyperlinks on our website may occasionally be out of date.

We do not own or publish the content on a manufacturer's website; therefore, the products we carry might sometimes differ in content, packaging, or technical specifications from those described online. If you have a specific requirement, please contact our sales team to confirm details.

All goods remain the property of IoTBay until payment has been received in full. Until title passes, IoTBay reserves the right to retain, repossess, and/or resell all goods. IoTBay also reserves the right to withdraw any products or specials listed on our website and to update prices and stock availability in accordance with market conditions without notice.
